inYanzeng Zhang is a Staff Scientist in the Applied Mathematics and Plasma Physics group in the Theoretical Division at the Los Alamos National Laboratory. Prior to this appointment, he was a Director's Postdoc Fellow in the same group. He received a Ph.D. in Engineering Physics from the University of California San Diego (UCSD) in 2020 and was awarded UCSD MAE Department Distinguished Fellowship. His research interests include modeling and simulating the plasma particle and power exhaust, thermal quench, plasma-neutral interaction, atomic physics, and turbulence.
